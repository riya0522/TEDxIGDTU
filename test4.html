Our TEDxIGDTU 2020 speakers

For our 2020 edition, we have a wide range of speakers, from diverse backgrounds, to share with us "The Unsaid" stories of their lives. We at TEDxIGDTU want this edition to be about the unsaid stories of trials and tribulations, of triumph, of happiness and tears, and about the hidden, often unexpressed side to your life journey.

TEDxIGDTU 2019 - Aftermovie

I feel honoured and privileged to have been a part of TEDxIGDTU 2019, where I got the chance to share my story. But above all, I was delighted to work with the organising team especially Disha and Hansika, who were very helpful with their ideas and suggestions. Not only do I believe that the event was very well organized from start to finish but I also believe that the speakers were pampered! Thank you, guys, for giving me the opportunity to share what I love doing the most. Wish you all every success in the future!
Naman Ahluwalia
TEDxIGDTU’19 Speaker
I was a part of the audience of TEDxIGDTU’19 and if I had to describe my entire experience in one word, it would be: exhilarating. I went to the talk with an empty mind ready to absorb all sorts of ideas and I was not disappointed. True to theme of the event, each talk was unique in its own way and I had something to gain from every one of them. It helped me to gain a lot of perspective, both at the individual and social level and I can say without a doubt, that attending TEDxIGDTU’19 has definitely had a positive and everlasting impact on my attitude towards everything.
Surmayi Sharma
TEDxIGDTU’19 Attendee
The second edition of TEDxIGDTU was organised in January'19 with the aim to spread ideas and thoughts, worth sharing. It was a brilliant experience being a part of the Organising Committee of the event. One thing that I would embrace forever is the team spirit and the devotion and effort which each one of us had put in to make the event a huge success. Being a part of Media and Communication team, I realised the power of media in disseminating information and to connect a wider section of community while Program Management team made me realise the importance of time management and effective team work to ensure an incessant flow of the event.
Sanjana Goel
TEDxIGDTU'19 OC Member
