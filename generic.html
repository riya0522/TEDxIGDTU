THE UNSAID

Not all words fit to their meanings, sometimes what is said is not what is meant, and what is meant is often left Unsaid.

These are opinions that were never voiced, ideas that were never brought to light, innovations that were never noticed, voices that were never heard. The realm of ‘The Unsaid’ is as vast as it is versatile and tapping into this diverse domain of unsaid experiences, of unspoken struggles of quiet determination, of unheard stories, and unexplained wonders, TEDxIGDTU brings to you a celebration of The Unsaid this 21st of March, 2020.

They say that what is left unsaid often says it all, so come join us as we all discover our unspoken, unsaid potentials together.


Say it with Unsaid.
